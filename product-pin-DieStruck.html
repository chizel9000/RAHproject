Getting Started

Fill out our quote form online 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, Or call our office during business hours. Please provide information about what youre looking for in your custom product. You will receive instructions from our team, a price quote and artwork in 24 business hours.

Payment Options

We can make less than 100 lapel pins for you but the unit price goes up quite a bit. The reason for this is simple. Pricing is based on the setup and production costs associated with a 100 quantity order. So, if you only order 20 pins, you still have to pay what it would cost to order a larger amount. That is why we suggest 100 pins as the minimum to get the manufacturing process started.

Creation Process

We actually do all of our artwork upfront and free of charge with unlimited revisions. Our art team can also take anything from a written description to a hand drawn sketch. So whatever you have we can take it and create a digital rendering of what your custom product will look like once produced.
